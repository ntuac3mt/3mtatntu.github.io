Saturday, 18th October, 2014, LT2A 11 AM - 2:30 PM

Promo Video:

From the team that brought you TEDxNTU...Join us out West, as we engage in dialogue and stimulating discussions on :

What's Wrong With Science?

  • Is modern science too profit driven?
  • Is research all about publications, impact factor and conferences?
  • When does popular science become pulp fiction?
  • What can be done to improve scientific research?
  • How should science be conveyed to the general public?

The discussion will be driven by various subject matter experts who will speak out of their experience and long acquired basket of wisdom, making way for an enlightening session. Come wear your thinking hats into this enchanting arena and get ready to dive into the depths of your intriguing thoughts!

THE EXPERTS

Assoc Prof Kwok Kian Woon, Associate Provost (Student Life), NTU
Diane Cmor, Deputy University Librarian
Asst Prof Teru Miyake, Philosopher of Science
Asst Prof Christopher Cummings, Science Communicator
More to be confirmed soon!
